Welcome to Brussels College

Brussels College is a private higher education institution in the heart of Europe. We deliver English‑taught undergraduate and postgraduate programmes with a clear international focus. In collaboration with UK institutions, undergraduate degrees are awarded by the University of Greater Manchester (UK); for integrated postgraduate pathways, final awards are conferred by the University of Greater Manchester (UK) through our delivery partner WINC.

Visa, Stay & Student Life in Brussels

Visa D (Long Stay)

Obtaining Your Visa

Apply with a valid passport, health insurance, enrolment certificate, and proof of funds (blocked account ~€835/month for 12 months). Typical processing ~3 months.

Local Renewal

Extend in Belgium

With your next admission letter from Brussels College, renew your stay locally without travelling home. Our Student Desk and your municipality (commune) guide the process.

Accommodation

Find a Place to Live

Our Orientation Desk supports you before arrival. Separate housing options available upon request. Typical living + food costs align with the blocked‑account amount (~€835/month).

Healthcare

Insurance & Support

Non‑EU students usually budget ~€25/month for health insurance, meeting Belgian legal requirements and covering major medical needs.

Student Status

Benefits & Discounts

Official student status unlocks reductions on public transport, cultural activities and local services across the city.

Student Jobs

Work While You Study

Students can work up to 20 hours/week during term (approx 650 hours/year reduced‑rate regime). Typical wages: €13–€18/hour. Our Student Desk offers CV guidance and job search support.

Career Office

From Study to Career

1:1 coaching, employer links and internship matching give you a strong start in Belgium’s vibrant economy across business, tech and services.
