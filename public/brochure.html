Visa & Student Life

Living & Studying in Brussels

Long Stay Visa

Type D Visa Application

International students require a Type D (long-stay) visa. Required documents include valid passport, health insurance, enrollment certificate, academic transcripts, and proof of funds.

Visa Renewal

Local Extension

Students can renew their visa locally in Belgium with their next admission letter from Brussels College.

No travel required back to home country for renewal process.
📋 Complete Visa Support: Our Student Desk provides comprehensive document checklists, application guidance, and renewal assistance | 📞 student.desk@brucol.be
