Student Life & Support

Student Visa & Stay

International students require a Type D visa (3-month processing). Our student desk provides comprehensive support throughout the process, and you can extend your visa locally in Belgium without returning home.

Reference: dofi.ibz.be

Accommodation

Our Orientation Desk helps secure accommodation before arrival. Student houses available with separate options for male/female students.
